## 13. Offline Data & Synchronization

### 13.1 Offline-First Architecture

Our mobile app is designed to work without constant internet connectivity:

- **Local Database:** WatermelonDB (SQLite) stores tasks, contacts, and operational data on your device
- **Offline Access:** View and edit data even without internet connection
- **Background Sync:** Automatic synchronization every 5 minutes when online
- **Manual Sync:** Pull-to-refresh to force immediate sync
- **Conflict Resolution:** Server data takes precedence (read-only task data from Salesforce)

### 13.2 Data Stored Locally on Device

The following data is stored in the app&apos;s local database:

- Task assignments and details
- Contact information (customers, team members)
- Form submission drafts
- Photos pending upload (temporary cache)
- Sync metadata and timestamps
- User profile information

### 13.3 Sync Behavior

- **Initial Sync:** Full data download upon first login or after clearing app data
- **Incremental Sync:** Only changed records synced after initial load
- **Network Awareness:** Sync pauses when offline, resumes automatically when connection restored
- **Sync Logs:** Activity logged for troubleshooting (retained 90 days)

### 13.4 Data Persistence

- **App Deletion:** Local data permanently deleted when app is uninstalled
- **Cache Clearing:** Use in-app &quot;Clear Cache&quot; option to remove local data (Settings &gt; Storage)
- **Logout:** Local data retained on device after logout (for faster re-login) - manually clear if sharing device

### 13.5 Security Recommendations for Offline Data

Since offline data is stored on your device:
- Enable device lock screen (PIN, pattern, fingerprint, or face recognition)
- Keep device OS updated with latest security patches
- Use &quot;Clear Cache&quot; before selling or disposing of device
- Report lost/stolen devices to privacy@earthenable.org immediately

## 14. Cookies & Tracking Technologies (Web Dashboard)

### 14.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our web dashboard. They help us recognize you, remember preferences, and provide secure access.

### 14.2 Cookies We Use

#### 14.2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies (Cannot Be Disabled)

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `ee_session` | Maintain authenticated session | Session (deleted on browser close) |
| `ee_auth_token` | Store JWT access token securely | 30 days or until logout |
| `ee_csrf` | Prevent cross-site request forgery (CSRF) attacks | Session |

#### 14.2.2 Functional Cookies (Optional)

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `ee_theme` | Remember light/dark mode preference | 1 year |
| `ee_lang` | Store language preference | 1 year |
| `ee_sidebar` | Remember sidebar expanded/collapsed state | 30 days |

**You can disable functional cookies:** These enhance experience but are not required. Disable in browser settings, though some preferences may not persist.

#### 14.2.3 Analytics Cookies (Future - Opt-In Required)

**Status:** Not currently implemented. When we add analytics tools (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.):
- You will see a cookie consent banner on first visit

### 14.3 Managing Cookies

#### 14.3.2 Consequences of Disabling Cookies

## 22. Definitions

For clarity, here are definitions of key terms used in this policy:

- **Personal Data/Information:** Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person
- **Processing:** Any operation performed on personal data (collection, storage, use, disclosure, deletion)
- **Data Controller:** Entity determining purposes and means of data processing (EarthEnable)
- **Data Processor:** Entity processing data on behalf of controller (Salesforce, AWS, etc.)
- **Data Subject:** The individual whose personal data is processed (you, the user)
- **Consent:** Freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous indication of your wishes
- **Pseudonymization:** Processing data so it can no longer be attributed to a specific person without additional information
- **Anonymization:** Irreversibly removing all identifiers so data can never be traced back to an individual
