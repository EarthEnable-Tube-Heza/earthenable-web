# Installation Guide

## Download and Install the EarthEnable Mobile App

This guide will walk you through the process of installing the EarthEnable mobile app on your Android device.

---

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- **Android device** running Android 8.0 (Oreo) or higher
- **At least 150MB** of free storage space
- **earthenable.org email address** (company email)
- **Internet connection** for initial download and setup
- **Access to Managed Google Play** (your device should be enrolled)

---

## Step 1: Access Managed Google Play

The EarthEnable app is distributed through **Managed Google Play** for internal use by EarthEnable staff only.

### On Your Android Device:

1. Open the **Google Play Store** app on your device
2. Look for the **"Work"** tab at the bottom of the screen (indicated by a briefcase icon)
3. Tap on the **"Work"** tab to access your organization's managed apps

![Managed Google Play Work Tab](/images/app-screenshots/managed-play-work-tab.png)

> **Note:** If you don't see a "Work" tab, your device may not be enrolled in Managed Google Play. Contact your IT administrator for assistance.

---

## Step 2: Find the EarthEnable App

Once you're in the Managed Google Play (Work tab):

1. Use the **search bar** at the top
2. Type **"EarthEnable"** or **"EarthEnable App"**
3. The app should appear in your search results with the EarthEnable logo

![Search for EarthEnable App](/images/app-screenshots/search-earth Enable.png)

> **Alternative:** Your IT administrator may have already pushed the app to your device. Check your app drawer or notifications for automatic installation.

---

## Step 3: Install the App

1. Tap on the **EarthEnable** app from the search results
2. Review the app permissions:
   - **Location** - Required for field operations tracking
   - **Storage** - Required for offline data storage
   - **Camera** - Optional, for future features
3. Tap the **"Install"** button
4. Wait for the download and installation to complete (typically 1-2 minutes)

![Install EarthEnable App](/images/app-screenshots/install-screen.png)

### Installation Size:

- **Download size:** ~50 MB
- **Installed size:** ~120 MB (including initial data)

---

## Step 4: First Launch & Sign In

After installation is complete:

1. Tap **"Open"** from the Play Store, or find the **EarthEnable** icon in your app drawer
2. The app will open to the **splash screen**
3. You'll be greeted with the **sign-in screen**

![Sign In Screen](/images/app-screenshots/sign-in-screen.png)

4. Tap the **"Sign in with Google"** button
5. Select your **earthenable.org email account**
   - If you have multiple Google accounts, choose the one ending in `@earthenable.org`
6. Grant the requested permissions when prompted

> **Important:** Only earthenable.org email addresses are authorized to access the app. Personal Gmail accounts will not work.

---

## Step 5: Initial Sync

After successful sign-in:

1. The app will perform an **initial sync** to download your tasks and data
2. You'll see a progress indicator showing "Syncing data..."
3. This may take **2-5 minutes** depending on:
   - Your internet connection speed
   - Amount of data to download
   - Number of tasks assigned to you

![Initial Sync Progress](/images/app-screenshots/initial-sync.png)

> **Tip:** Ensure you have a stable Wi-Fi connection for the initial sync. Subsequent syncs will be much faster.

---

## Step 6: Explore the App

Once the initial sync is complete, you're ready to start using the app!

You'll see the **Dashboard** with:

- **Task statistics** - Overview of your tasks by status
- **Quick actions** - Buttons to view tasks and access features
- **Sync status** - Last sync time and connection status

![App Dashboard](/images/app-screenshots/dashboard.png)

### Navigation:

The app has three main sections accessible from the bottom navigation:

1. **Dashboard** (Home icon) - Overview and statistics
2. **Tasks** (List icon) - View and manage your tasks
3. **Settings** (Gear icon) - App preferences and account settings

## Troubleshooting Installation Issues

### Issue: "App not compatible with your device"

**Cause:** Your Android version is too old.

**Solution:**

- Check your Android version: Settings → About phone → Android version
- Minimum required: Android 8.0 (Oreo)
- If below 8.0, contact IT for a device upgrade

### Issue: "Can't find the app in Managed Google Play"

**Cause:** Device not enrolled in Managed Google Play, or app not approved for your account.

**Solution:**

- Verify you're looking in the "Work" tab, not the regular Play Store
- Contact your manager or IT administrator
- Email: support@earthenable.org

### Issue: "Installation failed - Insufficient storage"

**Cause:** Not enough free space on device.

**Solution:**

- Free up at least 150MB of storage
- Delete unused apps or media files
- Move photos/videos to Google Photos or computer

### Issue: "Sign in failed - Invalid account"

**Cause:** Using non-earthenable.org email or account not authorized.

**Solution:**

- Ensure you're using your earthenable.org email
- Check with your manager that your account is activated
- Try signing out of all Google accounts and signing in again

### Issue: "Initial sync stuck or taking too long"

**Cause:** Poor internet connection or server issues.

**Solution:**

- Connect to a stable Wi-Fi network
- Close and reopen the app to retry sync
- If issue persists after 10 minutes, contact support

## Uninstall the App

If you need to uninstall the app:

1. Go to **Settings → Apps → EarthEnable**
2. Tap **"Uninstall"**
3. Confirm the uninstallation

> **Warning:** Uninstalling will delete all locally stored data. Make sure your latest changes have synced to the server before uninstalling.
