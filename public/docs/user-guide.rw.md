## Gutangira

### Porogaramu ya EarthEnable ni iki?

Porogaramu ya EarthEnable ni **igikoresho cyo gucunga imirimo** gishobora gukoreshwa hatabaye interineti. Ifasha abakozi muri Rwanda, Kenya, Zambia, n'Ubuhinde gucunga imirimo yabo, kuzuza ubushakashatsi, no gukurikirana iterambere—nubwo nta muhuza wa interineti.

### Ibintu by'Ingenzi:

- ✅ **Gucunga imirimo offline** - Kora ahantu hose, igihe cyose
- ✅ **Sync otomatiki** - Amakuru akoreshwa iyo uhujwe
- ✅ **Gukurikirana iterambere mu gihe nyacyo** - Kureba icyiciro cy'ibikorwa
- ✅ **Guhuza na FormYoula** - Kugera ku bushakashatsi mu buryo bworoshye
- ✅ **Indimi nyinshi** - Icyongereza n'Ikinyarwanda
- ✅ **Umutekano w'ubwiyunge** - Kwinjira kwa Google na konti ya sosiyete

## Kwinjira muri Porogaramu

### Kwinjira ku Nshuro ya Mbere

1. Fungura porogaramu **EarthEnable**
2. Uzabona aho **kwinjira** hamwe na logo ya EarthEnable
3. Kanda buto **"Sign in with Google"**
4. **Hitamo konti yawe:**
   - Hitamo imeri yawe `@earthenable.org`
   - Niba ufite konti nyinshi za Google, menya neza ko uhitamo konti ikora
5. **Emera uruhushya** iyo usabwe

> **Ngombwa:** Gusa imeri za earthenable.org zemerewe. Konti za Gmail z'umuntu ku giti cye ntizakora.

---

## Gusobanukirwa Dashboard

Nyuma yo kwinjira, uzagera kuri **Dashboard** - ikigo cy'imirimo yawe.

### Ibice bya Dashboard:

#### 1. **Umutwe**

- **Salut:** "Muraho, [Izina Ryawe]"
- **Igihe cya sync y'iherezo:** Yerekana igihe amakuru yakoreshejwe
- **Ikimenyetso cya sync:** Icyatsi (uhujwe), Orange (sync), Umutuku (offline)

#### 2. **Amakarita y'Imibare y'Imirimo**

Amakarita ane yerekana imirimo yawe:

- **Imirimo Yose:** Umubare wose w'imirimo wahawe
- **Ntiyatangiye:** Imirimo utaratangira
- **Iriho:** Imirimo ugendereye kuri ubu
- **Ryarangiye:** Imirimo warangije

---

## Kuraba no Gucunga Imirimo

Jya kuri tab **Imirimo** (icon y'urutonde) hasi kugira ngo ubone imirimo yawe yose.

### Amahitamo yo Gushungura Imirimo:

- **Byose** - Erekana imirimo yose
- **Ntiyatangiye** - Gusa imirimo utaratangira
- **Iriho** - Imirimo ugendereye kuri ubu
- **Ryarangiye** - Imirimo yarangiye
- **Gutegereza** - Imirimo itegereje isuzuma

Kanda chip yo gushungura kugira ngo werekane gusa imirimo ziri muri icyo cyiciro.

---

## Kurangiza Imirimo

### Kuvugurura Icyiciro cy'Umurimo

1. Kanda ku **ikarita y'umurimo** aho uri
2. **Task Detail Modal** ifungura
3. Shakisha igice cy'**"Update Status"**
4. Kanda **dropdown ya status**
5. Hitamo status nshya:
   - **Ntiyatangiye** → Iyo umurimo uhewe
   - **Iriho** → Iyo utangiye gukora ku murimo
   - **Ryarangiye** → Iyo urangije umurimo
   - **Gutegereza** → Bitegereje isuzuma
6. Kanda buto **"Update Status"** kugira ngo ubike

> **Icyitonderwa kuri Offline:** Ushobora kuvugurura status nubwo nta muhuza wa interineti. Impinduka zibikwa aho kandi zizakoreshwa otomatiki iyo umurongo ugarutse.

## Gukoresha FormYoula

Imirimo myinshi isaba kuzuza ubushakashatsi bwo gusuzuma ireme binyuze kuri FormYoula.

### Gufungura Ubushakashatsi

1. Fungura **ibisobanuro by'umurimo**
2. Kuzura mu gice cy'**"Quality Assurance Surveys"**
3. Uzabona urutonde rw'ubushakashatsi bufitanye isano (niba buhari)
4. Kanda buto **"Open Survey"** iruhande rw'ubushakashatsi ugomba kuzuza

## Gukoresha Offline & Sync

Porogaramu ya EarthEnable yashizweho **gukora offline**, bivuze ko ushobora kuyikoresha ahantu hose, nubwo nta muhuza wa interineti.

### Uburyo Offline Mode Ikora

**Iyo uri offline:**

- ✅ Kuraba imirimo yawe yose
- ✅ Gushungura no gushakisha imirimo
- ✅ Kuvugurura status y'umurimo
- ✅ Kuraba amakuru y'abakiriya
- ✅ Impinduka zose zibikwa aho kuri telefone yawe

**Ibisaba interineti:**

- ❌ Kwinjira kwa mbere
- ❌ Sync ya mbere nyuma yo gushyiraho
- ❌ Kumanura imirimo mishya wahawe
- ❌ Kohereza impinduka za status y'imirimo kuri seriveri

## Guhindura Ururimi

Porogaramu ifasha **Icyongereza** n'**Ikinyarwanda**.

### Guhindura Indimi

1. Jya kuri **Igenamiterere** (icon ya gear hasi)
2. Shakisha igice cy'**"Ururimi"**
3. Kanda **"Hitamo Ururimi"**
4. Hitamo:
   - **English** (Icyongereza)
   - **Kinyarwanda** (Ikinyarwanda)
5. Porogaramu izahita ihinduka mu rurimi wahisemo

---

## Kumenyesha Ibibazo

Wabonanye ikosa cyangwa ufite ibitekerezo? Koresha ibikoresho byo kumenyesha ibibazo.

### Uburyo bwo Kumenyesha Ikibazo

1. Jya kuri **Igenamiterere** → **Ubufasha & Inkunga**
2. Kanda **"Kumenyesha Ikibazo"**
3. **Ifishi yo Kumenyesha Ikibazo** ifungura
4. Uzuza ifishi
5. Kanda **"Kohereza Raporo"**
