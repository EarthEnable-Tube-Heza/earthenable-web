# Amabwiriza yo Gushyiraho Porogaramu

## Kuramo no Gushyiraho Porogaramu ya EarthEnable ku Mugaragaro

Iyi nyandiko izagufasha mu gushyiraho porogaramu ya EarthEnable kuri telefone yawe ya Android.

![Gushyiraho Porogaramu ya EarthEnable](/images/app-screenshots/install-screen.jpg)

### Intambwe z'Ihuse zo Kwishyiriraho:

1. **Ibisabwa Mbere yo Gutangira** - Menya ko ufite Android 7.0+, umwanya wa 150MB uhari, n'imeri ya earthenable.org
2. **Injira Ukoresheje Imeri Yawe ya EarthEnable** - Ongeraho konti yawe ya @earthenable.org kuri telefone yawe
3. **Fungura Google Play Store** - Hindura konti kugeza ku konti y'akazi yawe muri Play Store
4. **Shakisha Porogaramu ya EarthEnable** - Shakisha "EarthEnable" muri Play Store
5. **Shyiraho Porogaramu** - Kanda Install kandi utegereze kugeza gumanurwa birangiye
6. **Gufungura bwa Mbere & Kwinjira** - Fungura porogaramu kandi winjire ukoresheje Google
7. **Murakaza neza kuri EarthEnable!** - Reba dashboard n'ibindi biranga porogaramu
8. **Urubuga rwo Kuyobora** - Kubona igenamiterere n'ubundi bushobozi

> **Inama:** Niba ukeneye amabwiriza arambuye, komeza kuri slides zikurikira. Niba ataribyo, kurikiza intambwe hejuru!

---

## Ibisabwa Mbere yo Gutangira

Mbere yo gutangira, reba niba ufite:

- **Telefone ya Android** ikoresha Android 7.0 (Nougat) cyangwa irenga (API level 24+, Target SDK 36)
- **Byibuze 150MB** y'umwanya uhari
- **Imeri ya earthenable.org** (imeri y'ikigo) yashyizwe kuri telefone yawe
- **Umuhuza wa interineti** kugira ngo umanure kandi ushyireho porogaramu bwa mbere

---

## Intambwe ya 1: Injira Ukoresheje Imeri Yawe ya EarthEnable

Menya neza ko imeri yawe ya earthenable.org yashyizwe kuri telefone yawe ya Android:

### Kuri Telefone Yawe ya Android:

1. Jya kuri **Settings** → **Accounts**
2. Niba utabona imeri yawe ya **@earthenable.org** ku rutonde:
   - Kanda **"Add account"** → **"Google"**
   - Injira ukoresheje imeri yawe ya **earthenable.org** n'ijambo ryibanga
   - Rangiza uburyo bwo kwinjira

---

## Intambwe ya 2: Fungura Google Play Store

Porogaramu ya EarthEnable iraboneka muri **Google Play Store** kugira ngo ikoreshwe gusa n'abakozi ba EarthEnable.

### Fungura Play Store Ukoresheje Konti Yawe y'Akazi:

1. Fungura porogaramu ya **Google Play Store** kuri telefone yawe
2. Kanda **icon ya profil yawe** hejuru iburyo
3. Niba konti nyinshi zigaragaye, **kanda kugira ngo uhindure** kuri konti yawe ya **@earthenable.org**
4. Ubu ugomba kubona Play Store ukoresheje konti yawe y'akazi

![Guhindura Konti muri Google Play Store](/images/app-screenshots/play-store-account-switch.jpg)

> **Icyitonderwa:** Porogaramu yiboneka gusa mugihe winjiye ukoresheje konti yawe ya earthenable.org.

---

## Intambwe ya 3: Shakisha Porogaramu ya EarthEnable

Ukoresheje konti yawe y'akazi muri Play Store:

1. Koresha **agasanduku k'ubushakashatsi** hejuru
2. Andika **"EarthEnable"** cyangwa **"EarthEnable Apps"**
3. Porogaramu igomba kugaragara mu bisubizo by'ubushakashatsi hamwe n'ikimenyetso cya EarthEnable

![Shakisha Porogaramu ya EarthEnable](/images/app-screenshots/google-play-store-search-earthenable.jpg)

> **Inama:** Niba utabona porogaramu, menya neza ko winjiye ukoresheje konti yawe ya @earthenable.org muri Play Store.

---

## Intambwe ya 4: Shyiraho Porogaramu

1. Kanda kuri porogaramu ya **EarthEnable** mu bisubizo by'ubushakashatsi
2. Reba uruhushya rwa porogaramu:
   - **Aho uherereye** - Birakenewe kugira ngo ikurikirane ibikorwa byo mu murima
   - **Ububiko** - Birakenewe kugira ngo ubike amakuru ari offline
   - **Kamera** - Ntago birakenewe, ariko bizakoreshwa mu bikorwa bizaza
3. Kanda buto ya **"Install"**
4. Tegereza gumanura no kwishyiraho kurangira (mubisanzwe iminota 1-2)

![Shyiraho Porogaramu ya EarthEnable](/images/app-screenshots/install-screen.jpg)

### Ingano yo Kwishyiraho:

- **Ingano yo gumanura:** ~50 MB
- **Ingano nyuma yo kwishyiraho:** ~120 MB (harimo amakuru yambere)

---

## Intambwe ya 5: Gufungura bwa Mbere & Kwinjira

Nyuma yo kwishyiraho kurangira:

1. Kanda **"Open"** muri Play Store, cyangwa ushakishe ikimenyetso cya **EarthEnable** muri app drawer yawe
2. Porogaramu izafungura igaragaze **splash screen**
3. Uzasubirwa n'**ecran yo kwinjira**

![Ecran yo Kwinjira](/images/app-screenshots/sign-in-screen.jpg)

4. Kanda buto ya **"Sign in with Google"**
5. Hitamo **konti yawe ya imeri ya earthenable.org**
   - Niba ufite konti nyinshi za Google, hitamo iyisoza na `@earthenable.org`
6. Emera uruhushya rusabwa igihe byasabwe

> **Ngombwa:** Imeri ya earthenable.org gusa niyo yemewe kwinjira muri porogaramu. Konti za Gmail z'umuntu ku giti cye ntizakora.

---

## Intambwe ya 6: Murakaza neza kuri EarthEnable!

Nyuma yo kwinjira neza, uzagera kuri **Dashboard** - ibi biremeza ko kwishyiraho byarangiye!

### Icyo Uzabona kuri Dashboard:

**Igice cy'Hejuru:**

- **Ifoto ya profil yawe** - Ifoto ya konti yawe ya Google
- **Ikuzo ryihariye** - "Murakaza neza, [Izina Ryawe]!"
- **Guhindura ururimi** - Hindura hagati y'Icyongereza na Kinyarwanda
- **Ikimenyetso cy'indangagaciro** - Kanda ikimenyetso cy'umutima kugira ngo urebe "Murakaza neza kuri EarthEnable" n'indangagaciro

**Ibiri Hagati:**

- **Amakarita y'indangagaciro azunguruka** - Indangagaciro nkuru za EarthEnable zigaragara mu buryo buzunguruka
- **Amakarita y'ibiranga** - Amakarita yo kuyobora ku biranga porogaramu:
  - **Imirimo** (Irakora) - Reba kandi ukurikire imirimo washinzwe
  - **Ibindi biranga** (Bizaza vuba) - Ubundi bushobozi

![Dashboard ya Porogaramu](/images/app-screenshots/dashboard.jpeg)

> **Icyitonderwa:** Amakuru y'imirimo arahinduka mu buryo bwikora inyuma. Igihe ukanda **Akarita y'Imirimo** bwa mbere, akenshi kuvugurura byaba byarangiye.

---

## Intambwe ya 7: Urubuga rwo Kuyobora

Kanda **ifoto ya profil yawe** kuri icyerekezo cy'ibumoso hejuru kugira ngo ugaragaze urubuga rwo kuyobora.

### Amahitamo Aboneka:

- **Menyesha Ikibazo** - Tanga raporo z'ibibazo na tekinike hamwe na diagnostics ikora ku buryo bwikora
- **Verisiyo ya Porogaramu** - Reba numero ya verisiyo y'ubu n'amakuru y'ubwubatsi
- **Gusohoka** - Sohoka kuri konti yawe usubire ku ecran yo kwinjira

![Urubuga rwo Kuyobora](/images/app-screenshots/navigation-drawer.jpg)

### Byose Byarangiye!

Kwishyiraho byarangiye. Ubu ushobora gukora ibiranga bya porogaramu. Kugira ngo ubone amabwiriza arambuye ku buryo bwo gukoresha ibiranga runaka, reba **[Umuyoboro w'Ukoresha](/app-docs/user-guide)**.

## Gukemura Ibibazo byo Kwishyiraho

### Ikibazo: "Porogaramu ntabwo ihuye na telefone yawe"

**Impamvu:** Verisiyo ya Android yawe ni ya kera cyane.

**Igisubizo:**

- Reba verisiyo ya Android yawe: Settings → About phone → Android version
- Igipimo gisabwa: Android 7.0 (Nougat) - API level 24+
- Niba ari munsi ya 7.0, hamagara IT kugira ngo uvugurure telefone

### Ikibazo: "Ntushobora kubona porogaramu muri Google Play Store"

**Impamvu:** Utinjiye ukoresheje konti yawe ya earthenable.org, cyangwa porogaramu ntiyemerewe kuri konti yawe.

**Igisubizo:**

- Menya neza ko winjiye ukoresheje konti yawe ya **@earthenable.org** muri Play Store
  - Kanda icon ya profil yawe muri Play Store urebe konti ikora
  - Hindura kuri konti yawe ya earthenable.org niba bikenewe
- Shakisha "EarthEnable" cyangwa "EarthEnable Apps" (ijambo ryuzuye rikenewe)
- Niba igikomeza kutaboneka, hamagara umuyobozi wawe cyangwa IT administrator
- Imeri: support@earthenable.org

### Ikibazo: "Kwishyiraho byanze - Umwanya utahagije"

**Impamvu:** Umwanya utahagije kuri telefone.

**Igisubizo:**

- Kuraho byibuze 150MB y'umwanya
- Siba porogaramu zitakoreshwa cyangwa dosiye za media
- Imura amafoto/videwo kuri Google Photos cyangwa mudasobwa

### Ikibazo: "Kwinjira byanze - Konti itemewe"

**Impamvu:** Ukoresha imeri itari ya earthenable.org cyangwa konti itaremewe.

**Igisubizo:**

- Menya neza ko ukoresha imeri yawe ya earthenable.org
- Reba n'umuyobozi wawe niba konti yawe yarakozwe
- Gerageza gusohoka muri konti zose za Google hanyuma winjire ukundi

### Ikibazo: "Imirimo ntiyipakurwa cyangwa ntiravugurura"

**Impamvu:** Umuhuza wa interineti mubi cyangwa kuvugurura inyuma kurangira.

**Igisubizo:**

- Menya neza ko ufite umuhuza wa interineti ukomeye (Wi-Fi irasabwa)
- Kanda **Akarita y'Imirimo** kuri dashboard kugira ngo utangire kuvugurura mu buryo bw'intoki
- Gukurura hasi ku rutonde rw'imirimo kugira ngo uvugurure
- Niba imirimo igikomeje kutaboneka nyuma y'iminota mike, funga hanyuma ufungure porogaramu ukundi
- Hamagara ubufasha niba ikibazo kikomeje: support@earthenable.org

## Gukuraho Porogaramu

Niba ukeneye gukuraho porogaramu:

1. Jya kuri **Settings → Apps → EarthEnable**
2. Kanda **"Uninstall"**
3. Emeza gukuraho

> **Urubanza:** Gukuraho bizasiba amakuru yose yabitswe ku buryo bwaho. Menya neza ko impinduka zawe ziheruka zavuguriwe kuri server mbere yo gukuraho.

---

## Kuvugurura

Porogaramu yakira ivugurura binyuze muri Managed Google Play:

- **Kuvugurura ku buryo bwikora:** Byashyizwe mbere (bisaba Wi-Fi)
- **Kugenzura ivugurura mu buryo bw'intoki:** Play Store → My apps & games → Updates
- **Amenyesha y'ivugurura:** Uzamenyeshwa ivugurura rinini

Verisiyo nshya zisanzwe zirimo:

- Gukosora amakosa no kunoza imikorere
- Ibiranga bishya
- Ivugurura ry'umutekano
