## Konti & Kwinjira

### Ikibazo: Ni iyihe meri ngomba gukoresha kugira ngo ninjire?

**Igisubizo:** Ugomba gukoresha imeri yawe ya **earthenable.org** y'ikigo. Konti za Gmail z'umuntu ku giti cye cyangwa izindi meri ntizakora.

Urugero: `jean.mugabo@earthenable.org`

---

### Ikibazo: Ndabona "Sign in failed" error. Nkore iki?

**Igisubizo:** Gerageza izi ntambwe:

1. **Reba imeri yawe**: Menya neza ko ukoresha imeri yawe `@earthenable.org`
2. **Reba umuhuza wa interineti**: Usabwa interineti kugira ngo winjire bwa mbere
3. **Sohoka muri konti zose za Google**
4. **Siba cache ya porogaramu**
5. **Ongera utangire telefone yawe**
6. **Gerageza ukundi**

Niba ikibazo gikomeje, hamagara support@earthenable.org

---

### Ikibazo: Ninjiye neza, ariko ndabona "Account not authorized" error

**Igisubizo:** Ibi bivuze ko konti yawe ibaho muri Google ariko itarakora muri sisitemu ya EarthEnable.

**Ibisubizo:**

- Hamagara umuyobozi wawe kugira ngo amenye neza ko konti yawe yarashinzwe
- Umuyobozi wawe akeneye gukorera konti yawe muri dashboard ya admin
- Tegereza iminota 10-15 nyuma yo gukorera, hanyuma ugerageze kwinjira ukundi

## Imirimo & Gucunga Imirimo

### Ikibazo: Ntabwo mbona umurimo uwo ariwo wose. Ikibazo ni iki?

**Igisubizo:** Impamvu zishoboka:

1. **Nta murimo wahawe uyu munsi**: Umuyobozi wawe ashobora kutaba yarakugenereye imirimo
   - Hamagara umuyobozi wawe kugira ngo arebe gutanga imirimo

2. **Sync ntiyarangiye**:
   - Jya kuri Dashboard
   - Reba igihe "Cya sync ya nyuma"
   - Kanda "Sync Ubu" kugira ngo utangire sync

3. **Ibibazo byo guhuza mugihe wa sync ya mbere**:
   - Menya neza ko ufite umuhuza wa interineti uhamye
   - Gerageza sync ukundi

---

### Ikibazo: Ndashobora guhindura status y'umurimo ute?

**Igisubizo:** Kurikira izi ntambwe:

1. Jya kuri tab **Imirimo**
2. Kanda ku **ikarita y'umurimo** ushaka kuvugurura
3. **Task Detail Modal** ifungura
4. Shakisha igice cy'**"Update Status"**
5. Kanda **dropdown ya status**
6. Hitamo status nshya (Ntiyatangiye ‚Üí Iriho ‚Üí Ryarangiye ‚Üí Gutegereza)
7. Kanda buto **"Update Status"**

## Gukoresha Offline & Sync

### Ikibazo: Nshobora gukora iki iyo ndi offline?

**Igisubizo:** Iyo uri offline, ushobora:

‚úÖ **Kuraba imirimo yawe yose** - Imirimo yakoreshejwe iboneye
‚úÖ **Gushungura imirimo** - Ukurikije status
‚úÖ **Kuvugurura status y'umirimo** - Impinduka zibikwa aho
‚úÖ **Kuraba ibisobanuro by'umurimo** - Amakuru yose y'abakiriya

‚ùå **Ntushobora gukora:**

- Kumanura imirimo mishya yaguwe nyuma yo kuba offline
- Gufungura ubushakashatsi bwa FormYoula
- Kohereza impinduka za status y'umurimo kuri seriveri

---

### Ikibazo: Inshuro zingahe porogaramu ikorera sync amakuru?

**Igisubizo:** Porogaramu ikorera sync mu buryo bwikora muri izi nzego:

- **Buri munota 5** iyo uhujwe na interineti
- **Kuri porogaramu itangiye** (sync ya mbere iyo ufungura porogaramu)
- **Ako kanya nyuma yo kuvugurura status y'umurimo** (niba uri online)
- **Iyo umuhuza wasubijwe** nyuma yo kuba offline

Ushobora kandi **gutangiza sync n'intoki** igihe cyose:

1. Jya kuri Dashboard
2. Kanda buto **"Sync Ubu"**

## Guhuza na FormYoula

### Ikibazo: FormYoula ni iki kandi kuki nkeneye?

**Igisubizo:** FormYoula ni porogaramu itandukanye ya mugaragaro ikoreshwa mu **bushakashatsi bwo gusuzuma ireme** kuri EarthEnable.

**Kuki porogaramu itandukanye?**

- FormYoula yihariye mu bushakashatsi bugoye bufite amafoto, GPS, umukono
- Porogaramu ya EarthEnable yibanze ku gucunga imirimo

---

### Ikibazo: Nshobora gushyiraho FormYoula ute?

**Igisubizo:** FormYoula ishyirwaho uhereye kuri Google Play Store:

1. Iyo ukanze **"Open Survey"** ku murimo, uzajyanwa kuri Play Store
2. Niba itashyizweho, kanda **"Install"**
3. Tegereza igihe gishyirwaho
4. Garuka kuri porogaramu ya EarthEnable

## Ururimi & Localization

### Ikibazo: Nshobora guhindura ururimi rwa porogaramu ute?

**Igisubizo:** Kugira ngo uhindure hagati y'Icyongereza n'Ikinyarwanda:

1. Fungura porogaramu
2. Jya kuri **Igenamiterere**
3. Shakisha igice cy'**"Ururimi"**
4. Kanda **"Hitamo Ururimi"**
5. Hitamo:
   - **English** (Icyongereza)
   - **Kinyarwanda** (Ikinyarwanda)

## Imikorere & Ibibazo bya Tekiniki

### Ikibazo: Porogaramu ikora buhoro. Nshobora gukosora ute?

**Igisubizo:** Gerageza ibi bisubizo:

1. **Funga porogaramu ziri inyuma**
2. **Siba cache ya porogaramu**
3. **Ongera utangire telefone yawe**
4. **Reba umwanya wa telefone**
5. **Kuvugurura porogaramu**

---

### Ikibazo: Porogaramu yarasenyutse. Nkore iki?

**Igisubizo:** Niba porogaramu ifunga bidatunguranye:

1. **Ongera ufungure porogaramu** - Mubisanzwe iratangira neza
2. **Reba niba amakuru ari afite umutekano**
3. **Niba ikomeza gusenyuka** (inshuro 3+):
   - Ongera utangire telefone yawe
   - Siba cache ya porogaramu
   - Kumenyesha ikibazo binyuze mu Igenamiterere

## Amakuru & Ubwite

### Ikibazo: Amakuru yanjye afite umutekano?

**Igisubizo:** Yego. EarthEnable yita cyane ku mutekano w'amakuru:

‚úÖ **Isobekerezwa:**

- Amakuru yose yoherezwa binyuze kuri HTTPS
- Database ya SQLite isanzwe kubika amakuru aho

‚úÖ **Kwemeza:**

- Google OAuth
- JWT tokens hamwe na 30-day expiry

---

## Kuvugurura Porogaramu

### Ikibazo: Nshobora kuvugurura porogaramu ute?

**Igisubizo:** Ivugurura ritangwa mu buryo bubiri:

**1. Over-the-air (OTA) updates:**

- Ivugurura rito (gukosora amakosa, impinduka nto)
- Rimanuka mu buryo bwikora inyuma

**2. Ivugurura ryuzuye (Google Play):**

- Ibintu bishya bikomeye
- Impinduka nini
- Jya kuri Managed Google Play ‚Üí My apps & games ‚Üí Updates
